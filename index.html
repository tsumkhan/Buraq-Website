Why Buraq Space Camp?

Buraq Space Camp represents the pinnacle of youth development in Pakistan, combining cutting-edge space technology education with leadership training. Our intensive 10-day program transforms exceptional young minds into tomorrow's innovators and leaders.

Through hands-on projects, mentorship from industry experts, and collaborative challenges, participants develop not just technical skills, but the confidence and vision to tackle Pakistan's greatest challenges.
